My Story

I'm a Mechatronic Systems Engineering student at Rhine-Waal University of Applied Sciences in Germany. Mechatronics lives at the intersection of mechanical design, electronics, and software — and that mix is exactly what drew me to it. I'm less interested in a single part and more in how a whole system behaves.

My focus is simulation in mechatronics: modelling how mechanical and electronic systems move and respond before they're ever built. Alongside that, I work with CAD and design, programming, control systems, and embedded electronics.
